AT LLFITNESS, WE ARE ALL ABOUT HEALTH.

We provide you with a wide range of services, and while our methods are founded on the latest findings and developments in the industry, we always focus on one thing: The client. Helping you meet your individual needs and achieve your personal goals is at the heart of our organisation, and what drives each one of us.

Owner/CEO Lorenzo Lanzafame

My passion for fitness is something I live every day, whether as a personal trainer for my clients, as a coach for MMA fighters, or by training myself. I believe in inspiring people to make health a part of their lifestyle and I work hard to help my clients achieve their goals. This is my way of doing my part, and I hope it makes a difference to all the people we work with.

I founded LLFitness because I believe that exercise helps people become happier, healthier, and more confident - the best version of themselves.

Since then, LLFitness has grown to be a well-established company in Cambodia, and the first professional high-quality provider of personal training. Services now go far beyond personal training, and the team expanded to include other certified specialists such as our dedicated physiotherapist.

Offering a training experience that has not existed in Cambodia before, LLFitness created a space where clients are motivated and feel at home, with professional, passionate trainers. Working towards the client's goals is and always has been at the core our mentality, and the personal goals of our clients are and will always be the focus of the entire team.

We are proud to offer a comprehensive and results-driven service that supports our clients in achieving the results they wish for. We do more than delivering results - we create an environment that gives you the opportunity to grow and achieve the goals you set for yourself, to become better every day.

And that is why we love what we do.
